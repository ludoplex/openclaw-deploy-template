## üé® BRAND CONSISTENCY ELEMENTS

### Always Include:
- **Logo:** Mighty House Inc. logo in corner or footer
- **Colors:** Primary blue (#0066CC), Accent green (#00CC66), Energy orange (#FF6600)
- **Fonts:** Bold sans-serif headlines, clean body text
- **Contact:** Website/phone on all materials
- **Trust:** Include "Local" "Same Day" "Guaranteed" where applicable

### Photo Style Guide:
- Real people when possible
- Diverse representation
- Friendly, approachable faces
- Clean, organized workspaces
- Modern tech equipment
- Warm lighting

### Icon Style:
- Simple, modern line icons
- Consistent line weight
- Match brand colors
- Not cartoonish for professional services
- Slightly playful for gaming content
