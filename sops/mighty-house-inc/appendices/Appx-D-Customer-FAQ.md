## Frequently Asked Questions

### Repairs

**Q: How long will my repair take?**
A: Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. We will call you with an update and final cost before proceeding.

**Q: What if you find additional problems?**
A: We will contact you with a revised estimate. No additional work is done without your approval.

**Q: Do you guarantee your work?**
A: Yes. All labor carries a 30-day warranty. If the same issue returns within 30 days, we will fix it at no additional charge.

**Q: What happens if you cannot fix my device?**
A: If we cannot resolve the issue, you only pay the diagnostic fee (if applicable). We believe in honest assessments.

### Data and Privacy

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: We take data privacy seriously. Technicians only access what is necessary for the repair. We never copy, share, or browse personal files.

**Q: Can you recover my lost files?**
A: We offer data recovery services on a best-effort basis. No data recovered = no charge for that service.

### Pricing

**Q: Are your prices fixed?**
A: Yes. Our prices are posted and transparent. No hidden fees, no surprises.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Seniors (65+) and veterans receive 10% off labor Monday through Thursday.

### Gaming Center

**Q: What ages can use the gaming center?**
A: All ages are welcome. Players under 13 must have a parent/guardian sign consent.

**Q: Can I bring my own games?**
A: Our systems are pre-loaded with available games. Outside software is not permitted.
