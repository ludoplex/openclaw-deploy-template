## Emergency Procedures

### Fire

1. Alert customers and staff
2. Call 911
3. Evacuate via nearest exit
4. Meet at designated assembly point
5. Do NOT re-enter building
6. Account for all staff

### Medical Emergency

1. Call 911
2. Do NOT move injured person unless danger present
3. Provide first aid if trained
4. Clear area around patient
5. Direct emergency responders to location
6. Document incident after resolved

### Robbery/Threat

1. Remain calm
2. Comply with demands
3. Do NOT resist or chase
4. Observe details (appearance, vehicle)
5. Call 911 when safe
6. Do NOT touch anything
7. Lock doors, do not admit anyone except police

### Power Outage

1. Remain calm
2. Use flashlights (no candles)
3. Protect equipment with UPS
4. Secure cash register
5. Contact power company
6. Follow closing procedures if extended

### Severe Weather

1. Monitor weather alerts
2. Move away from windows
3. If tornado warning: shelter in interior room
4. Do not leave until all-clear
5. Check for damage before resuming operations
