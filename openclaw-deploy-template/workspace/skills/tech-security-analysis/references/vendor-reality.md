# Vendor Reality

## Economics Drive Behavior

**Valuation Pressure**
- Sector average: 13.3x EV/Revenue
- Cloud security: 21.7x premium
- Result: Feature expansion > deployment success

**Sales Structure**
- 1:5 OTE to quota ($200K comp = $1M new ARR required)
- Commissions favor new ACV over effective implementation
- Shelfware rate: 28-60% of purchased tools

## Platformization Reality

**Narrative**: Consolidation simplifies security
**Reality**: 
- Lock-in risk increases
- Platform components often underperform specialists
- Gartner: "where they can afford to eliminate best-of-breed functionality without significant drops in efficacy"

## Major Player Dynamics

**Palo Alto Networks**
- Strategy: Aggressive discounting to displace competitors
- CyberArk acquisition ($25B) for identity
- Reality: Premium pricing, lock-in, -14% Q4 2025 stock

**CrowdStrike**
- July 2024: Global outage proved single-vendor risk
- Reality: Lost minimal business despite severity (stickiness)
- Dominant EDR but attackers routinely use EDR-killers

**Market Concentration**
- Top 5 vendors: ~45% market share
- Consolidation accelerating via PE
- Thoma Bravo: $60B+ in cybersecurity holdings

## Analyst Ecosystem

- Magic Quadrant: legally "pure opinion"
- Non-paying vendors: 4 analyst interactions/year
- Paying clients ($60K+): weekly advisory access
- Positioning advantages unrelated to product quality
