## Utility Sector Characteristics

```
REGULATORY STRUCTURE:
├── State PUCs: Rate setting, service territory
├── FERC: Wholesale markets, transmission
├── NERC: Reliability standards
└── Model: Regulated monopoly (most)

UNION PRESENCE:
├── IBEW: Primary electric/gas
├── UWUA: Utility Workers Union
├── Teamsters: Some operations
└── Density: 20-30% (higher than avg)

INVESTOR-OWNED vs PUBLIC:
├── IOUs: ~70% of customers
├── Municipal: ~15%
├── Co-ops: ~13%
└── Federal (TVA, BPA): ~2%
```
