## CFIUS & Regulatory Framework

```
CFIUS PROCESS:
├── Committee: Treasury-led, multi-agency
├── Review: National security assessment
├── Authority: Block or force divestiture
├── Recent Blocks:
│   ├── TikTok (forced restructure)
│   ├── Nippon Steel-US Steel (pending)
│   └── Various Chinese tech deals
└── Trend: More aggressive review

SECTORS OF CONCERN:
├── Semiconductors
├── AI/Quantum
├── Critical infrastructure
├── Defense supply chain
├── Personal data (bulk)
├── Telecommunications
├── Ports/logistics
└── Real estate near military bases

REVERSE CFIUS (2024):
├── Rule: Treasury final rule Oct 2024
├── Effective: January 2, 2025
├── Scope: US outbound investment to China
├── Sectors: AI, semiconductors, quantum
├── Type: Notification or prohibition
└── Enforcement: Treasury, potential sanctions
```
