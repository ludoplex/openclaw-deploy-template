## 4. Content Generation Economics

### Cost to Produce 200 Quality Images

**Option A: Cloud GPU (RunPod/Vast.ai)**
- RTX 4090: ~$0.40-0.50/hour
- ~1 minute per quality image with proper workflow
- 200 images = ~4 hours of generation = **~$2**
- But add LoRA training, iteration, selection: **~$10-20 total**

**Option B: ComfyOnline**
- $10 = 12,000 credits
- 0.62 credits/second on 4090
- 200 images (assuming 60 sec each) = 12,000 credits = **$10**

**Option C: Local GPU (if you have one)**
- Electric cost only: Essentially free
- RTX 3070+ can generate ~1 image/minute with SDXL/Flux

### LoRA Training Costs

- **Cloud training**: $5-20 per LoRA on RunPod (1-2 hours on A100)
- **Dataset preparation**: Time cost — 50-100 reference images needed
- **Iteration**: Plan for 3-5 training runs to get style right
- **Total realistic LoRA budget**: $30-100

### Quality Considerations

- AI-generated pin-up quality is high now (SDXL, Flux, Illustrious models)
- **Character consistency** is the challenge — solved by LoRA
- Will need manual touchup on ~10-20% of images (hands, anatomy)
