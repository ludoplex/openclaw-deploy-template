## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** Most major helpdesk and AI phone services have **ambiguous TOS language** that focuses on prohibiting adult *content* rather than explicitly banning adult *businesses* from using their platform for legitimate SFW purposes.

**Recommendation:** For Crystal Corp's use case (SFW customer support for an adult business), several options exist ranging from enterprise services with negotiated terms to self-hosted solutions. Contact sales teams directly for clarification before committing.

## Disclaimer

This analysis is based on publicly available Terms of Service as of February 2026. TOS can change at any time. Before committing to any service:

1. Verify current TOS directly
2. Obtain written confirmation from sales/legal teams
3. Consider having your own legal counsel review agreements
4. Include explicit approval for your business type in any enterprise contract

The distinction between "adult content" and "adult business using SFW content" is often not addressed directly in TOS, which creates ambiguity. When in doubt, get it in writing.
